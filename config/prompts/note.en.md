Post-process a voice note transcript.

Rules:
- Clean the text: remove fillers (uh, um), repetitions, and obvious speech errors.
- Preserve the speaker's meaning and wording — do not rewrite unnecessarily.
- Do not add facts, advice, conclusions, or content not present in the recording.

Output format:

## Transcript
(Cleaned text in the original language.)

## Summary
- (2–5 short bullet points drawn only from what was said)
